FAQ:

1. What are the essential products I need for my cat?

To keep your cat happy and healthy, you’ll need:


1. A high-quality cat food (wet or dry)
1. A litter box with clumping or non-clumping litter
1. A scratching post or pad
1. A comfortable bed
1. Food and water bowls (preferably stainless steel or ceramic)
1. Toys for mental stimulation
1. A cat carrier for travel or vet visits
2. How do I choose the right cat food?

Look for food that is high in animal-based protein, low in carbohydrates, and free from artificial additives. Consider your cat’s age, breed, and any dietary restrictions when selecting food. Always consult your vet for the best recommendations.

3. How often should I clean my cat’s litter box?

You should scoop out waste daily and completely change the litter once a week (or more often if needed). A clean litter box prevents odor and keeps your cat happy.
